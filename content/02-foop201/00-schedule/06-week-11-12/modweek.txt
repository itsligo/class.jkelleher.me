Here we revisit important past topics and round out our treatment of a variety of minor aspects such as:
* UI controls - Expander, Radiobuttons, Combo-boxes, Group boxes, Slider
* Data binding - populating listboxes via _ItemsSource_, role of **ObservableCollection**
* Parsing folder and files
* Operators - unary, binary, ternary and look at operator overloading
* Closer look at **Switch** statement
* Use of **sender** parameter in event handlers
* Finer UI design and build
